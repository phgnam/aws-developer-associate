VPC Connectivity Options

VPC-to-VPC Connectivity:

  • VPC Peering: Connect 2 VPCs together
  • Must have non-overlapping IP ranges
  • NOT transitive (must create peering for each VPC pair)
  • Cross-account or cross-region support

VPC-to-AWS Services:

  • VPC Endpoints: Private access to AWS services
  • No internet required for AWS service access
  • Important for exam - private connectivity
  • Will see in future lectures

VPC-to-On-Premises:

  • Site-to-Site VPN: VPN over public internet
  • Quick setup (minutes)
  • Lower cost option
  • Direct Connect: Direct private connection
  • Takes 1+ month to establish
  • Higher cost, better performance
  • Private line to AWS
